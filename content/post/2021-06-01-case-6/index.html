Pleuroparenchymal Fibroelastosis (PPFE)

Notes


Definion: Idiopathic form of PPFE considered a form of idiopathic interstitial pneumonia

Epidemiology and Localization:
Wide age range (20-80)
Males and Females equally affected
Upper lobes predominantly affected

Microscopy:
Subpleural dense fibroelastosis.

Therapy and Prognosis:
Diverse outcome of idiopathic form, some progress rapidly and require transplantation, others show slow progression over decades.
