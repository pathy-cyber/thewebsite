Nodular lung disease in a 20 year old woman

Special stains


Grocott

What’s your diagnosis


Histoplasmosis

Notes


Agent:
Histoplasma capsulatum (misnomer, no capsule present)

Epidemiology: Endemic disease in North, Central, and South America, Africa, and Asia, but also cases reported from Europe.

Treatment:
Usually not necessary. In case of disseminated disease (e.g. in immunocompromised), antifungal treatment.

Microscopy:
Small, budding, intracellular yeast in tissue, 2 - 5 microns in diameter.
