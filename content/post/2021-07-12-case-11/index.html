Ganglioneuroma

Notes


Definion:
Benign neoplasm that originates from neural crest cells of sympathetic ganglia or adrenal medulla

Epidemiology and clinical features:
Usually not in children.
Posterior mediastinum commonly affected

Microscopy:
Schwann cells and Ganglion cells.
Ganglion cells may be dysmorphic.
Subtypes are: with mature ganglion cells and with maturing ganglion cells.

Therapy and Prognosis:
Benign.
